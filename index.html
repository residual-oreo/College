Our Story

Founded by Accident, Excellent by Design

Legend has it, our founder was just looking for a quiet place to read a book and accidentally started a world-class educational institution. We've been rolling with it ever since.

1965

The first classroom was established in what was later discovered to be a broom closet.

1998

The "Mystery Meat Monday" tradition begins in the cafeteria. It remains a mystery to this day.

2010

A campus squirrel is awarded an honorary degree in physics for its groundbreaking work in gravity defiance.
