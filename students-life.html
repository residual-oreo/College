A Campus Experience More Vibrant Than Your Highlighter Collection

Life at College is more than just lectures and late-night study sessions. It's about finding your people, discovering new passions, and making memories that will last longer than your student loan debt.

We believe that a balanced life is key. That's why we offer a dizzying array of activities designed to help you procrastinate on your assignments in a productive, character-building way.

Join the Fun

Student Organizations & Clubs

Competitive Napping Club

Master the art of the 20-minute power nap between classes. Advanced members can sleep through an 8 AM lecture undetected.

15+ Masters
Caffeine Connoisseurs

Explore the philosophical implications of single-origin vs. blended coffee. Meets daily, or whenever someone needs a cup.

20+ Aficionados
Squirrel Watching Society

Document the complex social hierarchies and daring acrobatic feats of our campus's most powerful residents. Nuts provided.

25+ Observers

Athletics & Mildly-Strenuous Recreation

Synchronized Library Chair Swiveling

A sport of grace, timing, and trying not to make too much noise. Points awarded for style and silence.

Extreme Frisbee Golf

The only sport where hitting the Dean's window is both a penalty and a legendary achievement.

Varsity Speed-Walking to Class

For those who hit snooze one too many times. We are the reigning regional champions.

Competitive Textbook Stacking

Test your engineering skills and the limits of gravity. The winner gets bragging rights and a serious backache.

Campus Facilities (The Important Stuff)

Student Housing

Cozy, comfortable rooms where you can store your ramen collection and question your life choices.

Dining Facilities

Featuring the legendary "Mystery Meat Monday" and a salad bar for when your parents visit.

The Library (aka The Nap Zone)

A quiet place for "studying." Also features an extensive collection of books, probably.

Recreation Center

A place to work off the stress of your exams and the calories from late-night pizza.
