How to Apply: Your First Quest

01

Submit Application

Fill out the forms. We promise it's less painful than a group project. Mostly.

02

Send Documents

Gather your transcripts, letters of recommendation, and a small, non-perishable offering for the Admissions Gods.

03

The Interview

A friendly chat where we make sure you're not a robot. Unless you're applying for the robotics program.

04

Await Your Fate

We'll send a decision letter via email, carrier pigeon, or a squirrel with a tiny scroll. Whichever is fastest.

What You'll Need

  • A completed application form (all fields, please!)
  • Official transcripts that prove you went to class
  • SAT/ACT scores (or a really good excuse)
  • Two letters of recommendation from people who like you
  • A personal essay about your life's greatest challenge (besides this application)
  • A $65 fee (to power our coffee machine)
  • A positive attitude and a healthy sense of humor

International students may need to provide proof they can survive our winters and understand our local slang.
